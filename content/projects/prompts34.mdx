## Overview

Prompts34 is a platform to share prompts in Turkish. We decided to
start with Turkish to make it more niche and focus on a specific target group.

---

## Why We Built This

I have another app for sharing quotes. I was working on creating a
Twitter bot that will post these quotes frequently. My friend just
came up with the idea why don't we do the same thing for prompts? Apparently right
now there are a lot of influencers making videos and reels about best prompts.

I immediately jumped in because I think this will grow with time. Maybe we might
help to increase efficiency of AI usage and help people who are using it.

---

## What It Does

Users can share and find prompts. For start I started to organize by
tags. My plan is adding semantic search and intelligent categorization.

Right now it's a simple MVP version, lacking a lot. But I will add features with time depending
on user requests and usage.

---

## Technical Highlights

- **Frontend**: Next.js
- **Backend**: Python
- **Database**: Postgres

## How We Work Together

First we share ideas, what to do next, what is important and what is not.

## Current State

Right now the app is working in production. It doesn't have any features other than
being able to create and share prompts. Also added some SEO pages to see user reactions
and events.
